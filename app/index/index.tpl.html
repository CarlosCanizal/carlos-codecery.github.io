Crowd Park A Mobile solution for Private and Public Parking.
WHAT IS CROWD PARK? Crowd Park is an app that allows customers to easily find parking in lots or the street.
FEATURES Crowd Park makes things easier and better for you and your customers.
Payment
EASY WAY TO COLLECT PAYMENT

Set the right prices for your zone or parking lot, and your customers can start to pay you through mobile phone. Easy for both ways.

Mobile
JUST A MOBILE PHONE IS REQUIRED

Not additional investment or infraestructure is required, only needs a mobile phone. Forget about park meters or ticket machines.

Time
MONITORING LOTS IN REAL-TIME

Allow to your customers easily find parking lots. You and your customers can check in real-time if there is any spot free. Don't waste time!

Location
CHANGE PRICES FOR EVENT OR ZONE

In a busy day, you could raise your prices. And on a slow day, you could repurpose your parking spots for food truck night.
