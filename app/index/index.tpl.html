Crowd Park Gamified and Crowdsourced Private and Public Parking.
WITH CROWD PARK Users find and pay for parking from their phone. Lot owners and cities manage and enforce parking with zero investment.
FEATURES Real time supply and demand. Simplified enforcemnt and collection.
Payment
EASY SETUP

Draw your lot or public spaces on a map. Set available spots and pricing schedule. Users check-in and pay from their phone. Easy.

Mobile
ZERO INFRASTRUCTURE

Users snap pictures of other cars' plates. If the car didn't pay, our system sends a ticket and the user that snapped the picture collects a bounty. Simple.

Time
REAL TIME STATISTICS

Users see current and predicted demand for parking and are guided to available parking space.

Location
VARIABLE PRICING

On busy days, raise prices. On slow days, repurpose your parking lot for food trucks, a basketball court or a farmers' market!
